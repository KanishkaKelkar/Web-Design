Who are we?

Prime Real Estate is Thane’s the largest and finest township that balances nature & luxuries. The township offers total 7 towers named Athena, Barca, Solitaire B & C, Senina, Canary, Cardinal and Eagleridge with 1, 2, 2.5 & 3 BHK apartments. Meticulously planned to accommodate your way of life, these residences are designed to seamlessly blend with the choices you make. It's time for your dreams to turn into reality with every inch put to optimum use and every corner replicating smartness, the diligent planning makes sure you don’t run out of comfortable moving space. You live the brilliance, every single day. The interiors are designed to maximize functionality and the greenery, the accessibility, the safety and the aspect of community living makes a perfect nest for your family.
Prime Real Group established in 1978 and has been one of the most premium real estate developers in India with more than 40 years of experience in this business. They have the vision of creating a new India with self-sufficient and enduring townships. Our Group is awarded as the Most Iconic Township at Mid-day Real Estate Icons Awards.
